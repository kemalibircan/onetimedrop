## The Copy Shop Challenge

You've got everything on your phone: the form you need, the ticket you booked, the CV you updated at 11pm. The copy shop has a computer, a printer, and a clerk waiting for you to email the file to an address you'll probably mistype.

There's a far better way.

## Method: Instant QR Code Transfer

Here's how to use [OneTimeDrop](/) at a copy shop in under a minute:

### On the copy shop computer
1. Open Chrome or any browser
2. Go to [onetimedrop.io](/)
3. An 8-digit code and QR code appear — don't touch anything else

### On your phone
4. Open your camera and scan the QR code
5. Tap the link that appears — it opens OneTimeDrop automatically
6. Tap **Select files** and choose your document
7. Wait 2–5 seconds for upload

### Back on the computer
8. Your file appears in the file list
9. Click **Download**
10. Open it and print

## What Documents Can You Transfer?

- PDF files (recommended for printing)
- Word documents (.docx)
- Image files (.jpg, .png, .heic)
- PowerPoint presentations

> **Pro tip:** If you haven't already, convert your document to PDF before transferring. PDFs print exactly as intended, regardless of what software the copy shop computer has.

## How to Convert to PDF on Your Phone

**iPhone:** Open the document → Share → Print → Pinch to zoom the preview → Tap share again → Save as PDF

**Android (with Google Chrome):** Open the document → Share → Print → Select "Save as PDF"

**Google Docs:** Tap the three-dot menu → Share & Export → Send a copy → PDF

## Privacy Checklist for Public Computers

- [ ] Use incognito/private mode if possible
- [ ] After printing, delete the downloaded file from the Downloads folder
- [ ] Close the OneTimeDrop session (or wait for auto-expiry in 10 min)
- [ ] Don't let the computer save passwords or autofill data

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** OneTimeDrop files are automatically deleted after 10 minutes. However, the downloaded copy remains on the computer's hard drive until manually removed. Always delete your file from the computer after printing.

## FAQs for Copy Shop Use

**Q: What if the copy shop computer can't open my file format?**  
A: Convert to PDF first on your phone. PDFs are universally supported.

**Q: Can I send multiple files to print a whole document with images?**  
A: Yes, select multiple files at once in the upload picker.

**Q: What if the copy shop's computer has no internet?**  
A: OneTimeDrop requires internet. In this case, ask if they have a USB drive you can use to transfer from their computer, or use email as a fallback.

**Q: Is OneTimeDrop safe on a public computer?**  
A: The transfer is safe (session token protected), but the downloaded file will remain on the computer until deleted. Follow the privacy checklist above.
