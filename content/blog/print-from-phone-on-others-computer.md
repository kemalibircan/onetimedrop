## The Copy Shop Problem

You arrive at a print shop with a document saved on your phone. The clerk points at a computer. Your phone doesn't share a network with it. You don't have a USB drive. You don't want to log into your email on a public machine.

Sound familiar? This is one of the most common frustrations in daily digital life, and the solution is simpler than you think.

## The Easiest Way: Browser Session Code

Tools like [OneTimeDrop](/) put a session code and QR code on the screen of the computer you want to print from. You scan it with your phone, upload your document, and download it to the computer — ready to print.

No accounts. No USB cables. No emailing yourself.

## Step-by-Step at a Print Shop or Library

### On the computer (copy shop)

1. Open a browser and go to [onetimedrop.io](/)
2. A large QR code and 8-digit code appear on screen
3. Don't close this tab — keep it visible

### On your phone

4. Open your Camera app (iPhone/modern Android) and scan the QR code
5. Or go to [onetimedrop.io/join](/join) and type the 8-digit number
6. Tap **Select files** and choose your document (PDF, Word, etc.)
7. Wait for the upload to complete (seconds)

### Back on the computer

8. The file appears in the file list
9. Click **Download** — file saves to the desktop
10. Open it and print normally

## What File Types Can I Send for Printing?

- ✅ PDF — best choice for printing
- ✅ DOCX (Word)
- ✅ PPTX (PowerPoint)
- ✅ Images: JPEG, PNG, HEIC

Always prefer **PDF** for printing — it preserves formatting regardless of which software opens it.

## Privacy at Public Computers

> ⚠️ **Disclaimer:** OneTimeDrop files auto-delete after 10 minutes. However, be cautious on public computers. Files you download may remain in the Downloads folder until manually deleted. Always delete downloaded files from shared computers after printing.

### Safety checklist for public computers
- Use Private/Incognito mode when possible
- Delete your downloaded file from Downloads after printing
- Don't enter passwords or personal data
- Close all browser tabs when done

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Q: What if the copy shop computer can't open PDFs?**  
A: Most modern computers have PDF support built in. Alternatively, print directly from Chrome's built-in viewer.

**Q: What if the computer doesn't have internet?**  
A: OneTimeDrop needs internet on both devices. If the computer has no internet, ask the shop if there's a USB option available.

**Q: How do I convert my file to PDF on my phone?**  
A: On iPhone: use the "Print to PDF" shortcut in any app. On Android: most Office apps let you export as PDF directly.

**Q: Is the 8-digit code safe?**  
A: The code is randomly generated and expires in 10 minutes. Only the person with the code can join your session.
